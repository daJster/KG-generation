EconSearch Hub is a collaborative project led by students from ENSEIRB-MATMECA, Bordeaux INP. In partnership with the Bordeaux Economics Laboratory, we aim to create a knowledge graph generation tool from scientific articles in the field of economics.

EconSearch Hub empowers users to explore and understand economic insights efficiently. Whether you're a student, researcher, or industry professional, our tool provides a visual representation of economic knowledge extracted from scholarly articles, aiding informed decision-making.
